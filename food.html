From the history and consequences of food-borne illnesses or even death, most US manufacturers and retailers work swiftly to comply with recalls whether mandated by FDA or not.

When an FDA-regulated product is either defective or potentially harmful, recalling that product or removing it from the market or correcting the problem or is the most effective means for protecting the public.
